Top Landmarks

Tangadan Falls

A stunning waterfall with crystal-clear water where visitors can swim and enjoy the natural surroundings.

Baluarte Lighthouse

A scenic lighthouse offering panoramic views of the La Union coastline. Perfect for sunset photos.

Ma-Cho Temple

A Taoist temple in San Fernando, La Union, known for its beautiful architecture and cultural significance.

Baluarte Watchtower

Historical watchtower built during the Spanish colonial period, overlooking the coast.
