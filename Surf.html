Surfing in La Union

Ride the waves at the Surfing Capital of the North

Why Surf in La Union?

La Union is becoming the go-to destination for surfing enthusiasts in the Philippines. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced surfer, the waves here are perfect for all levels. With beautiful beaches, surf schools, and a nice surf community, you can enjoy both adventure and relaxation.

Top Surf Spots

San Juan is the most famous surfing spot, but other areas like Urbiztondo Beach offer great waves for beginners. Don't forget to check local tide charts for the best experience.

Surf Schools & Rentals

Learn from certified instructors or rent boards and gear directly from the beach. There are many surf schools offering lessons for kids, beginners, and advanced surfers.
