Must-Try Foods

Bagnet

Crispy deep-fried pork belly, a must-try specialty in La Union and northern Philippines.

Longganisa

Sweet and garlicky local sausage perfect for breakfast with rice and eggs.

Halo-Halo

Refreshing dessert with shaved ice, milk, sweet beans, fruits, and local toppings.

Refreshing Drinks

Fresh Coconut Juice

Direct from the tree, coconut juice is a refreshing way to beat the tropical heat.

Buko Shake

Blended coconut water and meat for a creamy, refreshing drink popular on beaches.

Calamansi Juice

Tangy and sweet, this citrus juice is a staple for locals and tourists alike.
